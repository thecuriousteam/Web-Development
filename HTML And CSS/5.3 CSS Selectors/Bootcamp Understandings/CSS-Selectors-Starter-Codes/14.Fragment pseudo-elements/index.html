Fluffy Pancakes Recipe

Looking for the perfect breakfast? These fluffy pancakes are quick to make and taste delicious. Below you'll find the ingredients and instructions to cook up a delightful meal for your family and friends.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Add eggs, milk, and melted butter; stir until well combined.
  3. Cook on a hot griddle or frying pan until golden brown.
